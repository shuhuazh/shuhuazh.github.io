Shuhua Zhang is currently pursuing a Ph.D. in the School of Automation at Nanjing University of Science and Technology. She received her MEng degree from Qingdao University of Science and Technology under the supervision of Prof. Ronghu Chi. Her research focuses on data-driven control, distributed control and estimation, networked nonlinear systems, and autonomous vehicles. Detailed information about her research projects, funding sources, honors, awards, and related publications is provided on this page.
